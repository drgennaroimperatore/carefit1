4: Managing time

What do I need to know?

  1. Caregiving is a massive achievement in itself and it can be extremely busy - there are over 6 million carers in the UK alone!
  2. CareFit is an app designed to recognise this, and our activities developed can be done in 5 minutes or less
  3. It might be that you have to think quite creatively about how to find the time- it could even be when the kettle is boiling!
  4. "Active living" is something that is important to remember - you are likely to be keeping fit already by some things you are already doing - and you can record it on CareFit.
  5. All of the activities on CareFit can be done from home and do not need specialised equipment.
