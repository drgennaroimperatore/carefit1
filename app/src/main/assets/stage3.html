3: Relationships and Physical Activity

What do I need to know?

  1. Relationships are a really important part of physical activity plans- both the way you feel and the support you may have from others.
  2. Many carers find that plans work well when they have had time to weigh up the benefits (pros) vs. the cons (barriers) to physical activity.
  3. Most carers will have a number of different barriers to face that are personal to them.
  4. Taking time to think of ways to address barriers to physical activity has been shown to be beneficial to those starting out in physical activity.
  5. Overcoming barriers is personal, you might be able to handle it on your own or you might need help from family or friends.
