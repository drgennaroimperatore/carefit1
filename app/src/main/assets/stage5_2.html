5: Goal Setting and Weekly Planner

Weekly Planner Development

To make your weekly plan we are going to:

  • Summarise your weekly goals overall
  • Develop more detailed plans for activity week 1
  • Develop more detailed plans for activity week 2

Developing your weekly goals

Not sure where to begin? You can refer back to activities you are doing on a daily basis. 

In the first part of the planner we are going to plan ahead for activity weeks 1 and 2. What do you think is realistic within week 1 and what can you comfortably increase for week 2?

Example of goals
