Physical Activity

Stage 2 Page 1: What does Physical Activity mean to you?

Physical activity means movement of the body that uses energy. It might make you think of running, cycling or playing sports but physical activity can take place at any level of skill, experience and setting.

What might be less obvious is that it can also take place within our daily work or home life, which experts call, “Active Living”. Active living includes housework, gardening, carrying the shopping, using stairs rather than lifts and caring roles.
