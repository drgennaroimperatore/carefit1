2: Physical activity, a beginner’s guide

What do I need to know?

  1. Physical activity is any movement of the body that uses energy.
  2. A balanced physical activity plan is one that includes: muscle and balance, sedentary and cardiovascular activity.
  3. For cardiovascular activities it is important to measure not just the time spent but the rate of effort- where you can use the talk test.
  4. When designing any physical activity plan its important to recognise how much you are doing and to make a plan that you feel comfortable with.
  5. Governments across the UK have developed national guidelines on physical activity.
