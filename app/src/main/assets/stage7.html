Stage 7: The mind and body

What do I need to know?

  1. There is a strong connection between the mind and body- physical activity is associated with a number of positive mental benefits.
  2. Because this connection is so strong, the mind plays an important role in our motivation and interest in physical activity- sometimes it can feel like it is working against us!
  3. It is extremely important to be kind to yourself throughout your physical activity journey- something is better than nothing.
  4. It’s ok not to achieve your goals every day.
  5. Many people find that meditation and mindfulness is helpful to overall wellbeing so we have included an example here for you to try.
