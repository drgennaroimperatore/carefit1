Stage 6: Physical Activity and consequences

What do I need to know?

  1. Making a plan for physical activity is a massive accomplishment- and it’s important to recognise this.
  2. Some benefits you might see quite quickly, but others will take time.
  3. Many caregivers will face setbacks along the way- things will not always go to plan.
  4. There are things you can do to help plan ahead for setbacks, including planning preventative and recovery strategies.
  5. Tricks to help you stay on track are personal to you- CareFit will help you organise and store this information to come back to whenever you need it.
