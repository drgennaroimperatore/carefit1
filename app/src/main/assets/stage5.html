Stage 5: Goal Setting and Weekly Planner

What do I need to know?

  1. Setting goals is an important part of starting out in regular physical activity. Any plans you make must reflect your current levels of physical activity.
  2. CareFit will allow you to set goals for activity week 1 ahead so you can plan your efforts around strength and balance, sedentary and cardiovascular activities.
  3. You will also be able to plan your activities for week 2 where you could make small increases in your activities.
  4. You will be able to add further details of the activities undertaken as you go and review progress.
  5. Carefit also offers a reminders and social media sharing function.
